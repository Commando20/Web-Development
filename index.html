Tic-Tac-Toe

Here we will play a classic game: Tic-Tac-Toe. You can either click to read and understand the rules of the game or go straight to playing!

Rules Play

Here's what you need to know!

  1. One player will play as an X and another player will play as an O.
  2. You will each take turns clicking on a 3x3 grid and attempting to fill in three boxes in a row (diagonally, horizontally, or vertically,).
  3. The first player to get three boxes in a row wins!
  4. But if all 9 boxes are filled and no player has three boxes in a row, the game shall end in a tie!
